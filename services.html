At MASEKO Cape Town, we offer tailored fashion solutions designed to meet your individual needs with style, precision, and excellence.

Custom Garment Design

We bring your vision to life through fully pieces for special occasions, graduations, formal events, or everyday statement wear. Each garment is made-to-measure and uniquely designed to suit your body, style, and story.

Tailoring & Alterations

As your trusted “Clothes Doctor,” we offer expert alteration services to give your garments the perfect fit and renewed life. From resizing to zip replacements, we handle every detail with care and precision.

Repairs & Restorations

We revive worn or damaged garments through professional repairs that extend their lifespan without compromising quality or appearance — ideal for sentimental pieces or premium fabrics.

Ready-to-Wear Pieces

Explore our limited collections of ready-made designs that reflect the bold and modern identity of MASEKO Cape Town. All pieces are rooted in African craftsmanship and urban elegance.

Pattern Making & Cutting (By Request)

We provide technical fashion services for fellow designers or students who need help with pattern drafting, sample cutting, or garment construction.
